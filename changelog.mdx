## How to Update

Follow these steps to apply the latest version to your wartenmitadana setup.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Current Version" icon="info">
    Log in to your dashboard at `https://app.wartenmitadana.de`.
    
    Navigate to **Settings > About** to view your installed version.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Update" icon="download">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Web Dashboard" icon="monitor">
        Click **Update Available** banner and follow prompts.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Terminal" icon="terminal">
````bash
# SSH into terminal
ssh admin@your-terminal-ip

# Run update
sudo wartenmitadana-update
````
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart Services" icon="refresh-cw">
    Restart the patient call service and verify on the TV display.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable auto-updates in your dashboard settings to receive new versions automatically. Back up your patient data before manual updates.
</Callout>
